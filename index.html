FAQ

Common questions

What does OJ Solutions actually do?
We help organisations execute complex change. That includes post-deal integration, divestments, operational redesign, technology transition, and enterprise AI transformation.
Do you only work on M&A projects?
No. M&A remains a core strength, but we also support broader transformation programmes where operations, systems, and decision-making need to be redesigned.
What do you mean by enterprise AI?
We mean AI applied to real business operations. That includes workflow automation, internal visibility, reporting, intelligent routing, decision support, and connected platforms.
Do you provide strategy or implementation?
Both. We define the right transformation path, then move it into structured execution.
Who do you typically work with?
Organisations navigating complexity — usually where multiple teams, systems, stakeholders, and risks need to be coordinated properly.
How do engagements begin?
Most begin with a brief, a discovery conversation, and a structured scoping process.

About

OJ Solutions exists to help organisations execute change properly

We work where complexity, risk, and business pressure meet. Our role is to bring structure, accountability, and practical movement to transformation.

OJ Solutions was built around a simple truth. Important change often breaks down in execution, not intent.

A strategy may be strong. A transaction may be compelling. A transformation case may be well argued. But when ownership is unclear, systems are disconnected, and stakeholders move without structure, value starts leaking fast.

That is why OJ Solutions focuses on the execution layer. We help organisations move through mergers, separations, technology transitions, process redesign, and enterprise AI transformation with more clarity and stronger control.

We are delivery-minded by design. That means we care about what happens after the plan is approved, after the decision is made, and after the pressure starts building.
